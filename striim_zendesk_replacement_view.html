Zendesk Replacement View (Future-State Planning)

CS planning page for a potential full replacement after hybrid validation. This captures what must be true before Striim can safely move from parallel mode to full DevRev-first support.

Target State: Full Platform Replacement Current State: Zendesk renewed for 12 months Gate: Legal, feature parity, migration confidence

Why this page exists

  • Hybrid evaluation is active; replacement is not immediate.
  • CS needs a forward plan to convert validated hybrid wins into replacement readiness.
  • This page reframes all gathered requirements through a cutover lens.

Support Tier Model for Full Replacement

  • T1: First-line customer support, AI-assisted responses, intake and triage.
  • T2: Complex troubleshooting, escalations management, workflow ownership.
  • T3: Engineering via JIRA-linked escalations and RCA automation.

Replacement Readiness Gates (Must Pass)
  1. BAA fully executed and all HIPAA/PHI restrictions validated in production workflows.
  2. Critical workflow parity complete for 20-30 Zendesk triggers (including role/time logic).
  3. Zendesk/JIRA integration stability proven under real volume with no major data integrity gaps.
  4. Unified timeline requirement resolved (native solution or accepted workaround).
  5. Chat widget fully activated and adopted in Striim production environment.
  6. UAT boolean checklist passes at agreed threshold ([ TBD ] threshold owner: Jason/leadership).
  7. Support team readiness across T1/T2/T3 with training and operational runbooks.
  8. Commercial model validated (license counts, AI action forecast, dual-stack cost-to-single-stack path).
